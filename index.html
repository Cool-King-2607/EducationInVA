How is Education in Virginia?

In recent years, Virginia has been increasing the standard of education by increasing funding to schools and teachers because Virginia has historically fallen behind the national average in funding for education.

The Numbers

$7,849,230,965 is the amount of money in the Virginia Budget for Education, that is enough to buy 8 luxury cruise ships

Virginia has around 1.3 million registered students, the teacher to students ration is on average 15:1

The average grade of a student in Virginia is an 86 or a B, this is 2 more than the national average of an 84

What does this all mean?

Virginia has been one of the fastest rising states in terms of support for education & teachers in the U.S.
